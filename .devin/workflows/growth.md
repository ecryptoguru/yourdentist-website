# /growth - Growth Strategy

$ARGUMENTS

---

## Purpose

This command builds a growth plan tied to funnel bottlenecks, measurable experiments, and platform-native channel execution.

---

## Primary Routing

- Primary agent: `marketing-strategist`
- Supporting agents when needed: `seo-specialist`, `product-manager`, `backend-specialist`, `data-engineer`
- Primary skills: `growth-marketing`, `social-media-patterns`

---

## When to Use

Use this workflow for:

- acquisition and activation strategy
- experiment backlog creation
- X, LinkedIn, or Reddit strategy
- campaign planning tied to business goals
- retention or funnel bottleneck analysis
- cross-channel content planning

---

## Workflow

1. Define the business goal
   - acquisition
   - activation
   - retention
   - referral
   - monetization

2. Identify the funnel bottleneck
   - where users are dropping
   - what evidence supports it
   - what baseline metrics exist

3. Build the strategy
   - target segment
   - channel choice
   - experiment ideas
   - content pillars
   - response/community expectations

4. Pull in specialists if needed
   - `seo-specialist` for search + GEO alignment
   - `product-manager` for prioritization and roadmap fit
   - `backend-specialist` or `data-engineer` for instrumentation/data constraints

5. Deliver a measurable plan
   - hypothesis
   - metric
   - timeframe
   - owner
   - guardrails

---

## Output Format

```markdown
## Growth Plan

### Goal
- [business outcome]

### Bottleneck
- [funnel constraint]

### Experiments
- [hypothesis + metric + timeline]

### Channel Plan
- [platform-specific actions]
```

---

## Rules

- Do not optimize for vanity metrics as the primary goal
- Do not cross-post identical content across platforms
- Ask clarifying questions first if audience, goal, or funnel stage is unclear
- Tie every recommended action to a measurable business outcome

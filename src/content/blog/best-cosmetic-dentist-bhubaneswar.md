## What does a cosmetic dentist do?

A cosmetic dentist focuses on improving the appearance of teeth, gums, and smiles. Common cosmetic treatments include:

- Teeth whitening
- Veneers and crowns
- Composite bonding
- Gum contouring
- Smile makeovers
- Clear aligners
- Tooth replacement with implants

A good cosmetic dentist also ensures that cosmetic work does not harm oral health or function.

## What makes a great cosmetic dentist?

### Technical skill

Cosmetic dentistry requires precision in tooth preparation, shade matching, and bonding. Small mistakes can affect both appearance and function.

### Artistic sense

Creating a beautiful smile is like art. The dentist must understand proportions, symmetry, and how teeth complement the face.

### Experience

Experience matters because cosmetic cases often require custom planning. An experienced dentist has handled a wide range of cases and knows what works.

### Technology

Digital smile design, intraoral cameras, and high-quality lab materials help achieve predictable results.

### Patient communication

The dentist should listen to your goals and explain what is realistically possible.

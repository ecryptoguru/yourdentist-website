## The orthodontic consultation process

### First visit

The first consultation includes:

- Discussion of concerns and goals
- Clinical examination
- Digital X-rays
- Photographs of teeth and face
- Impressions or digital scans

### Treatment planning

Dr. Dash analyses the records and creates a treatment plan. This includes:

- The type of braces or aligners recommended
- Estimated treatment time
- Cost estimate
- Appointment schedule
- Expected results

### Active treatment

Brackets or aligners are placed. Regular adjustments or aligner changes follow. Patients are guided on oral hygiene and care throughout.

### Retention

After active treatment, retainers are provided. Follow-up visits monitor stability.

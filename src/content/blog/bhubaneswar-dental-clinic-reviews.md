## What to look for when reading dental clinic reviews

| What to check | Why it matters | Red flags |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Number of reviews | More reviews = more reliable data | Very few reviews |
| Review dates | Recent reviews reflect current quality | All reviews from years ago |
| Response to negative reviews | Shows how clinic handles issues | Defensive or no responses |
| Specific procedure mentions | Confirms experience with your needed treatment | Vague, generic praise |
| Reviewer details | Local patients with similar needs | Anonymous, overseas reviewers |
| Star rating trend | Consistent high ratings over time | Sudden spikes or drops |

Take time to read multiple reviews before choosing a dental clinic. Patterns are more reliable than individual reviews.

## Types of braces available in Bhubaneswar

There are four main types of orthodontic treatment available at most clinics:

1. **Metal braces:** Traditional stainless steel brackets and wires.
2. **Ceramic braces:** Tooth-coloured brackets that are less visible.
3. **Self-ligating braces:** Braces with a sliding mechanism instead of elastic ties.
4. **Clear aligners:** Removable, transparent trays.

Each type has different costs, aesthetics, and treatment times.

## Factors that affect braces cost

### Complexity of the case

Simple cases with minor crowding cost less than complex cases with severe crowding, crossbite, or jaw problems. Complex cases need more appointments, longer treatment, and sometimes additional appliances.

### Type of braces

Metal braces are the most affordable. Ceramic and self-ligating braces cost more. Clear aligners are usually the most expensive because they are custom-made in a lab.

### Duration of treatment

Longer treatment means more appointments and adjustments. Most braces treatments last 12 to 24 months.

### Dentist's experience

An orthodontist or experienced dentist may charge more than a general dentist. Dr. Arpita Dash has extensive experience in orthodontic treatment with braces and aligners.

### Clinic location and facilities

Clinics with modern equipment and central locations may have slightly higher fees. Bhubaneswar prices are generally lower than metro cities.

### Retainers and follow-up

Retainers are needed after braces. Some clinics include retainer costs in the package, while others charge separately. Ask what is included in the quoted price.

## Factors that affect cosmetic dentistry cost

### Procedure type

Simple treatments like bonding and whitening cost less. Complex treatments like veneers, crowns, and smile makeovers cost more.

### Materials used

Porcelain, zirconia, and E-max materials cost more than composite or metal-ceramic. Higher-quality materials also last longer and look more natural.

### Number of teeth

Treating more teeth increases the total cost. Most smile makeovers cover 6 to 10 front teeth.

### Dentist experience

An experienced cosmetic dentist like Dr. Arpita Dash may charge more, but the results are usually better and longer-lasting.

### Technology

Digital smile design, laser gum contouring, and CAD-CAM crowns may add to the cost but improve precision and results.

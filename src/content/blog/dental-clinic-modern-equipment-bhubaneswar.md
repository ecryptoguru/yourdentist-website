## Why modern dental equipment matters

- **Less pain:** Better anesthesia delivery and laser-assisted procedures reduce discomfort.

## Digital X-rays and imaging

Traditional film X-rays take time to develop and expose patients to more radiation. Digital X-rays produce images instantly on a computer screen, use up to 90% less radiation, and can be enlarged for better diagnosis.

At YourDentist, we use digital X-rays for routine checkups, root canal diagnosis, implant planning, and orthodontic assessment. The images are stored digitally, making follow-up comparisons easy.

## CBCT scans for advanced planning

Cone Beam Computed Tomography, or CBCT, is a 3D imaging technology that shows teeth, bone, nerves, and sinuses in detail. It is especially useful for:

- Dental implant placement
- Wisdom tooth assessment
- Root canal complexity analysis
- Jawbone evaluation
- Orthodontic planning

CBCT helps Dr. Arpita Dash plan procedures with accuracy and avoid complications.

## Rotary endodontics for root canals

Rotary endodontic instruments are flexible, motor-driven files that clean root canals more efficiently than traditional hand files. They reduce the time needed for root canal treatment and cause less pressure on the tooth. This makes single-sitting root canals possible for most patients.

## Laser dentistry systems

Lasers are used at YourDentist for several procedures:

- **Root canal disinfection:** Laser energy kills bacteria in narrow canal branches.
- **Gum contouring:** Reshapes gums without scalpels or stitches.
- **Soft tissue surgery:** Removes growths and treats gum disease with less bleeding.
- **Teeth whitening:** Activates whitening gel for faster results.

Laser treatment often reduces pain, swelling, and healing time.

## Intraoral cameras

An intraoral camera is a small camera that shows the inside of your mouth on a screen. It helps patients see what the dentist sees, such as cavities, cracks, and gum problems. This makes explanations clearer and builds trust.

## Dental chairs with patient comfort features

Modern dental chairs are designed for patient comfort during long procedures. They recline smoothly, have adjustable headrests, and allow the dentist to work efficiently without causing strain to the patient.

## Autoclave sterilisation

Sterilisation is one of the most important parts of dental care. At YourDentist, we use autoclave sterilisation to kill bacteria, viruses, and spores on instruments. We also use disposable items wherever possible, such as gloves, masks, suction tips, and bibs.

## CAD-CAM technology for crowns

CAD-CAM, or computer-aided design and computer-aided manufacturing, allows crowns to be designed and milled in a single visit. This reduces the number of appointments needed for crown placement and improves the fit of the restoration.

## 3D printing in dentistry

3D printing is increasingly used for dental models, surgical guides for implants, and some types of aligners. It allows precise customisation and faster turnaround for certain procedures.

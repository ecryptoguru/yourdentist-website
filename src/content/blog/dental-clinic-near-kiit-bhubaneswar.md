## Common dental problems among KIIT students

- **Cavities:** Irregular meal times, sugary snacks, and soft drinks contribute to tooth decay.
- **Wisdom tooth pain:** Common in young adults aged 18 to 25.
- **Stained teeth:** Tea, coffee, and tobacco use darken teeth.
- **Gum problems:** Poor brushing habits lead to gingivitis.
- **Accidental tooth injuries:** Sports and hostel life can cause chipped or broken teeth.

## Dental care tips for KIIT students

1. Brush twice a day, even during late study nights.
2. Rinse your mouth after eating snacks or drinking sugary beverages.
3. Do not ignore tooth pain. It usually gets worse and costs more later.
4. Use a mouthguard during sports.
5. Visit a dentist every six months, even if you have no pain.
6. Avoid using tobacco and limit energy drinks.

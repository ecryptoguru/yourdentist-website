## Why dental treatment costs vary in Bhubaneswar

There is no fixed rate card for dental treatment in any Indian city. Several factors move the number up or down.

### The complexity of the case

A front tooth with one root canal is simpler than a molar with four curved canals. A routine cleaning takes 30 minutes; a deep scaling with gum treatment can take over an hour. Complex cases need more time and skill, so they cost more.

### Materials and brands

A zirconia crown costs more than a porcelain-fused-to-metal crown. A premium implant brand costs more than a basic one. At YourDentist, we explain the material options and let you choose based on your budget and how long you want the restoration to last.

### Technology used

Laser-assisted root canal treatment, digital smile design, and CAD-CAM crowns cost more than conventional methods. They also tend to be more precise, faster, and more comfortable. Whether the premium is worth it depends on your case.

### Dentist experience

A dental surgeon with 10 years of experience and thousands of completed cases will usually charge more than a fresh graduate. Dr. Arpita Dash has completed over 10,000 procedures in Bhubaneswar, and her pricing reflects that level of expertise without being inflated.

## Tips to manage dental treatment costs in Bhubaneswar

1. **Do not delay treatment.** A small cavity that costs ₹1,000 to fill can become a ₹5,000 root canal.
2. **Ask for a treatment plan in stages.** Large procedures like full mouth implants can be spread across several months.
3. **Compare value, not just price.** A cheap root canal with poor filling can fail and cost more later.
4. **Schedule regular checkups.** A six-month scaling prevents gum disease and expensive surgery.
5. **Choose the right material.** Zirconia is not always necessary. For back teeth, all-ceramic may be enough.

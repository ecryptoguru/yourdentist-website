Finding the right dentist in Bhubaneswar is not just about the nearest clinic. It is about finding someone qualified, experienced, and trustworthy who can take care of your teeth for years. With so many dental clinics in the city, it helps to have a clear checklist.

In Bhubaneswar, the number of dental clinics has grown significantly, but the quality of care varies widely. Some clinics lack modern equipment, proper sterilisation, or experienced specialists. Taking the time to evaluate your options carefully can prevent poor treatment outcomes and unnecessary expenses.

This 10-point checklist will help you choose the best dentist in Bhubaneswar for yourself and your family.

## 1. Check the dentist's qualifications and experience

A qualified dentist should have a recognised dental degree such as BDS or MDS. Experience matters, especially for complex procedures like implants, root canals, and orthodontics. Dr. Arpita Dash at YourDentist has over 10 years of experience and has completed more than 10,000 procedures.

Ask how long the dentist has been practising and whether they have handled cases similar to yours.

## 2. Look at the range of services

A good dental clinic should offer more than just fillings and extractions. Check if they provide:

- Root canal treatment
- Dental implants
- Braces and aligners
- Teeth whitening
- Crown and bridge
- Paediatric dentistry
- Gum treatment
- Oral surgery

Having multiple services under one roof saves time and ensures coordinated care.

## 3. Check patient reviews and ratings

Read reviews on Google, JustDial, and Practo. Look for patterns over time. A few negative reviews are normal, but repeated complaints about the same issue are a warning sign.

YourDentist has a 4.9-star rating based on more than 150 reviews, with consistent praise for painless treatment and clear communication.

## 4. Visit the clinic for cleanliness and hygiene

Sterilisation and cleanliness are non-negotiable. During your first visit, notice whether:

- The reception and waiting area is clean
- Instruments are opened from sealed packets
- Staff wears gloves and masks
- Surfaces are sanitised
- Waste is disposed of properly

A clean clinic reflects professional standards.

## 5. Ask about technology and equipment

Modern equipment improves diagnosis, comfort, and results. Ask if the clinic uses:

- Digital X-rays
- Rotary endodontics
- Laser dentistry
- Autoclave sterilisation
- Intraoral cameras
- CBCT scans for implants

YourDentist uses all of these technologies.

## 6. Evaluate communication and transparency

A good dentist explains the problem, the treatment options, and the costs before starting. You should not feel pressured or confused. Ask for a written estimate and take time to decide.

## 7. Consider location and timing

Choose a clinic that is easy to reach and has hours that suit you. If you work or study, evening and weekend appointments are important. YourDentist is in Bomikhal, central Bhubaneswar, and is open Tuesday to Sunday with morning and evening slots.

## 8. Check emergency availability

Dental emergencies can happen anytime. Ask if the clinic handles emergencies and how quickly they can see you. YourDentist treats emergencies during clinic hours and provides phone guidance after hours.

## 9. Compare costs, but do not choose only on price

The cheapest option is not always the best. Low-cost clinics may cut corners on materials or sterilisation. Compare the value you get for the price. Ask about:

- What materials are used
- Whether follow-up visits are included
- Warranty or guarantee on crowns and implants
- Payment options

## 10. Trust your comfort level

Finally, choose a dentist you feel comfortable with. If you feel rushed, judged, or ignored, it is okay to look elsewhere. A good dentist-patient relationship is based on trust and respect.

## Red flags to avoid

Watch out for these warning signs:

- No visible sterilisation process
- The dentist refuses to answer questions
- Prices that are much lower than other clinics without explanation
- Pressure to commit to expensive treatment immediately
- Outdated equipment and dirty surroundings
- No proper billing or receipts

## How to start your search

1. Ask friends and family for recommendations.
2. Search online for dentists near your area.
3. Read reviews on multiple platforms.
4. Visit the clinic once before committing to major treatment.
5. Ask questions and compare two or three clinics.

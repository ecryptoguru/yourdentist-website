## What does a laser dental clinic do?

A laser dental clinic uses focused light energy to perform dental procedures. Depending on the wavelength, lasers can cut soft tissue, remove decay, disinfect root canals, whiten teeth, and treat gum disease. The laser works without direct physical contact, which means less vibration, less bleeding, and less discomfort.

Lasers are not a replacement for all traditional tools, but they make many procedures safer and more comfortable.

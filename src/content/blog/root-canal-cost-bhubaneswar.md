## What is included in root canal cost?

A complete root canal treatment usually includes:

- Consultation and diagnosis
- Digital X-rays
- Local anesthesia
- Pulp removal and canal cleaning
- Canal filling and sealing
- Temporary filling
- Post-operative care

The final crown is usually priced separately because the crown material affects the cost significantly.

## Factors that affect root canal cost

### Number of canals

A tooth with more canals takes more time and skill to clean thoroughly. Molars typically have 3 to 4 canals, while front teeth have 1.

### Complexity of the infection

If the infection is severe, with swelling or abscess, more time and medication may be needed. Retreatment of a previously failed root canal also costs more because of the additional work.

### Technology used

Clinics using rotary endodontics and laser-assisted disinfection may charge more than clinics using basic hand files. However, these technologies improve comfort and success rates.

### Dentist experience

An experienced endodontist or dental surgeon with many root canal cases may charge more than a general dentist. The higher fee often reflects better outcomes.

### Location of the clinic

Clinics in premium locations may have slightly higher overhead costs. Bhubaneswar prices are generally lower than metro cities.

## Crown cost after root canal

After a root canal, the tooth becomes weaker and usually needs a crown. Crown costs depend on the material:

| Crown material | Cost range (INR) | Durability |
| --- | --- | --- |

| All-ceramic | ₹8,000 - ₹12,000 | 10 to 15 years |
| Zirconia | ₹12,000 - ₹18,000 | 15+ years |
| Metal crown | ₹3,000 - ₹5,000 | Very durable, less aesthetic |

The total cost of root canal plus crown in Bhubaneswar typically ranges from ₹7,000 to ₹28,000.

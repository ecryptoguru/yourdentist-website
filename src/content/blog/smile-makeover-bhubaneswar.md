## What is a smile makeover?

A smile makeover is a treatment plan that may include:

- Teeth whitening
- Veneers or crowns
- Orthodontic alignment (braces or aligners)
- Gum contouring
- Tooth replacement with implants
- Composite bonding
- Replacement of old fillings or crowns

The goal is to improve the colour, shape, alignment, and overall appearance of your smile.

## Who is a candidate for a smile makeover?

A smile makeover is suitable for anyone who wants to improve their smile. Common concerns include:

- Discoloured or stained teeth
- Chipped or broken teeth
- Gaps between teeth
- Crooked or uneven teeth
- Worn or short teeth
- Missing teeth
- Gummy smile
- Old, visible fillings or crowns

## Smile makeover treatments explained

### Teeth whitening

Professional whitening brightens the overall tooth colour. This is often the first step in a smile makeover.

### Veneers

Thin porcelain shells bonded to the front of teeth to improve colour, shape, and alignment. Veneers are the most common smile makeover treatment for front teeth.

### Crowns

Ceramic or zirconia caps that cover the entire tooth. Used when teeth are damaged or need structural support.

### Orthodontic alignment

Braces or clear aligners straighten teeth before veneers or crowns are placed. This ensures the final result is properly aligned.

### Gum contouring

Laser reshaping of the gum line to fix a gummy smile or uneven gums. This is a quick, painless procedure.

### Dental implants

Replace missing teeth with permanent, natural-looking artificial teeth.

### Composite bonding

Tooth-coloured resin applied to repair chips, gaps, or discolouration. A quick and affordable option for minor improvements.

## Caring for your smile makeover

- Brush twice daily with a soft brush
- Floss daily
- Avoid staining foods and drinks (coffee, tea, red wine)
- Do not bite hard objects
- Wear a nightguard if you grind your teeth
- Visit the dentist every 6 months
- Avoid smoking

We provide custom-made full and partial dentures in Bhubaneswar that restore your smile and chewing ability. Our dentures are designed for comfort and a natural look, using advanced materials that mimic the appearance of real gums and teeth.

## What are Dentures?

Dentures are removable prosthetic devices that replace missing teeth and surrounding tissues. Complete dentures replace all teeth in an arch, while partial dentures fill gaps when some natural teeth remain. Modern dentures are more comfortable, durable, and natural-looking than ever before.

## Types of Dentures Available

At YourDentist Laser Dental Clinic, we offer complete dentures, partial dentures, flexible dentures, and implant-supported overdentures. Each type is custom-fabricated to fit your mouth precisely. Flexible dentures offer superior comfort without metal clasps, while implant-supported dentures provide maximum stability.

## Getting Used to Dentures

It typically takes 2-4 weeks to adjust to new dentures. Initially, you may experience slight discomfort, increased salivation, and difficulty speaking. These effects are temporary. We provide detailed care instructions and schedule follow-up adjustments to ensure a perfect fit.

## Cost of Dentures in Bhubaneswar

Denture costs in Bhubaneswar range from ₹10,000 for basic complete dentures to ₹40,000 for premium flexible or implant-supported options. The price depends on the type, material, and number of teeth being replaced.

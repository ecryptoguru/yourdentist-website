Our crown and bridge services in Bhubaneswar restore damaged teeth and replace missing ones with durable, natural-looking prosthetics. We use high-quality materials like ceramic and zirconia for long-lasting results that blend seamlessly with your natural teeth.

## What is a Dental Crown?

A dental crown is a cap that covers a damaged or weakened tooth, restoring its shape, size, strength, and appearance. Crowns are recommended after root canal treatment, for cracked teeth, or for teeth with large fillings that lack structural support.

## What is a Dental Bridge?

A dental bridge replaces one or more missing teeth by anchoring artificial teeth to adjacent natural teeth or implants. Bridges prevent remaining teeth from shifting, restore your ability to chew properly, and maintain your facial structure.

We restore damaged teeth at YourDentist Laser Dental Clinic in Bhubaneswar using tooth-colored fillings, inlays, and onlays. Our restorations are both functional and aesthetic, ensuring your teeth look natural while being fully protected from further damage.

## What is Dental Restoration?

Dental restoration repairs teeth damaged by decay, fracture, or wear. The most common type is a filling, where decayed tooth material is removed and replaced with a durable material. Inlays and onlays are larger restorations used when a filling isn't sufficient but a crown isn't needed.

## Tooth-Colored Fillings

We use composite resin and glass ionomer materials that match your natural tooth color, making restorations virtually invisible. Unlike traditional silver amalgam fillings, tooth-colored fillings bond directly to the tooth structure, providing better support and a seamless appearance.

## Why Timely Restoration Matters

Untreated cavities progress deeper into the tooth, eventually reaching the pulp and causing pain and infection. Early restoration prevents the need for more extensive treatments like root canals or extractions. Regular dental check-ups at our Bhubaneswar clinic help detect cavities early.

## Cost of Dental Restoration in Bhubaneswar

Dental restoration costs in Bhubaneswar range from ₹1,000 for basic fillings to ₹5,000 for inlays and onlays. The cost depends on the material used, the size of the restoration, and the tooth's location.

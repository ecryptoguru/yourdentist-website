## What is Laser Dentistry?

Laser dentistry uses concentrated light beams to treat a variety of dental conditions. Dental lasers can be used on both soft tissue (gums) and hard tissue (teeth), offering a minimally invasive alternative to traditional dental tools.

## Advantages of Laser Dental Treatment

Laser dentistry offers several benefits over conventional methods: minimal bleeding due to natural cauterization, reduced need for anesthesia, faster healing times, lower risk of infection, and greater precision. Many patients experience less anxiety knowing that laser treatments are quieter and gentler than drills.

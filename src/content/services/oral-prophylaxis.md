## What is Oral Prophylaxis?

Oral prophylaxis, commonly known as professional teeth cleaning, is a preventive dental procedure that removes plaque, calculus (tartar), and surface stains from teeth. It includes scaling, polishing, and fluoride treatment to maintain optimal oral health.

## Why Regular Cleaning Matters

Even with excellent brushing and flossing habits, plaque can harden into tartar in hard-to-reach areas. Only professional scaling can remove tartar buildup. Regular cleaning every six months prevents gum disease, bad breath, and tooth decay, and allows early detection of dental issues.

## What to Expect During Your Visit

At our Bhubaneswar dental clinic, a typical cleaning appointment takes 30-45 minutes. Dr. Arpita Dash performs a thorough oral examination, followed by ultrasonic scaling to remove tartar, polishing to remove surface stains, and fluoride application to strengthen enamel.

## What is Orthodontic Treatment?

Orthodontic treatment corrects misaligned teeth and jaws using braces or clear aligners. It improves both the function and appearance of your smile, making it easier to clean your teeth and reducing the risk of dental problems in the future.

## Braces vs Clear Aligners

Traditional braces use metal brackets and wires to gradually move teeth into position. They are effective for all types of alignment issues, including complex cases. Clear aligners are transparent, removable trays that offer a discreet alternative for mild to moderate cases. During your consultation at our Bhubaneswar clinic, Dr. Arpita Dash will recommend the best option for your needs.

## Treatment Duration

Orthodontic treatment typically takes 12-24 months depending on the complexity of your case. Regular adjustment appointments every 4-6 weeks ensure steady progress. After treatment, retainers are worn to maintain the results.

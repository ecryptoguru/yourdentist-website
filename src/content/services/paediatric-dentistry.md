## Why Pediatric Dental Care Matters

Early dental care sets the foundation for a lifetime of healthy smiles. The American Dental Association recommends that children have their first dental visit by their first birthday or within six months after their first tooth erupts. Regular check-ups prevent cavities, monitor dental development, and establish good oral hygiene habits.

## What is Root Canal Treatment?

Root canal treatment (RCT) is a dental procedure that saves a severely damaged or infected tooth by removing the infected pulp, cleaning the root canals, and sealing them. Despite its reputation, modern root canal treatment is virtually painless thanks to advanced anesthesia and laser-assisted techniques.

## Recovery and Aftercare

After your root canal treatment, you may experience mild sensitivity for a few days. Avoid chewing on the treated tooth until the permanent crown is placed. Maintain regular oral hygiene and attend follow-up appointments to ensure proper healing.

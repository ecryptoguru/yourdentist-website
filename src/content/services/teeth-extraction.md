When a tooth cannot be saved, we perform extractions at YourDentist Laser Dental Clinic in Bhubaneswar with the utmost care and precision. Using modern anesthesia and gentle techniques, we ensure the procedure is as comfortable as possible with minimal recovery time.

## When is Tooth Extraction Necessary?

Tooth extraction may be required for severely decayed teeth that cannot be restored, impacted wisdom teeth, teeth with advanced periodontal disease, or teeth that are fractured below the gum line. Dr. Arpita Dash will always explore all options to save your tooth before recommending extraction.

## Wisdom Tooth Extraction

Wisdom teeth often cause problems when they don't have enough space to erupt properly. Impacted wisdom teeth can cause pain, infection, and damage to adjacent teeth. We perform wisdom tooth extractions with advanced techniques that minimize discomfort and swelling.

## What to Expect

The extraction procedure is performed under local anesthesia, ensuring you feel no pain during the process. Most extractions take 20-40 minutes. After the procedure, we provide detailed aftercare instructions to promote quick healing and prevent complications.

## Recovery After Extraction

Initial healing takes 1-2 weeks. We recommend rest, soft foods, and avoiding strenuous activity for the first 24 hours. Follow our aftercare instructions carefully to prevent dry socket. We schedule a follow-up to monitor healing and discuss tooth replacement options if needed.

## What is Professional Teeth Whitening?

Professional teeth whitening is a cosmetic dental procedure that removes stains and discoloration from teeth using concentrated whitening gel activated by laser or LED light. Unlike over-the-counter products, professional whitening delivers dramatic results safely and quickly.

## Why Choose Professional Whitening in Bhubaneswar?

At YourDentist Laser Dental Clinic, our laser-assisted whitening can brighten your smile by up to 8 shades in a single 45-minute session. The procedure is supervised by Dr. Arpita Dash, ensuring safety and optimal results. We protect your gums and use controlled concentrations to minimize sensitivity.

## How Long Do Results Last?

With proper care, professional teeth whitening results last 1-3 years. Avoiding coffee, tea, red wine, and tobacco helps maintain your brighter smile. We recommend periodic touch-up treatments and good oral hygiene practices.
